# FUTURE_DS_01
BUSINESS SALES PERFORMANCE ANAYTICS

This project analyzes business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, profitable categories, and regional performance. Using Power BI, an interactive dashboard was created to transform raw data into meaningful insights that support data-driven business decisions. The goal is to simulate a real-world business analytics scenario and provide actionable recommendations.
The analysis shows steady revenue growth over time with peak sales in specific periods. The Technology category generates the highest revenue and profit, while some categories show lower profit margins despite strong sales. A small number of top products contribute significantly to overall revenue. Regional analysis highlights strong-performing regions as well as areas with potential for growth.
The dashboard successfully converts sales data into clear business insights. It helps identify growth opportunities, improve profitability, and support strategic planning. This project demonstrates how data analytics can enhance decision-making and business performance through structured analysis and visualization.
